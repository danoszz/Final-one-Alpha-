The Haarlemse Honkbalweek (Haarlem Baseball Week) is an international invitation baseball tournament in Haarlem, Netherlands. It was first held in 1961 and has been held every other year in even-numbered years since 1972. It's the biggest baseball event in the Netherlands. I did the development in Wordpress, with some neat little features. And offcourse; it's responsive and fast loading!
