    Spotify's Colorful New Brand Identity

    Chris Dickman Fri, 03/13/2015 - 09:24

    It's true that Spotify's look had been getting pretty tired, so what better venue to release an updated identity than the latest incarnation of the buzz-generating South by Southwest (SXSW) festival, of which contemporary music plays such a central role. The event kicked off today and Spotify will have a very strong presence, marked by a brand refresh created by the Collins brand consultancy, on display at Spotify House.

    The familiar "dot" has been retained but transformed via a much more dynamic color palette, with Spotify apparently trying to shift perception of it from that of a tech company to the broader promise of an entertainment brand.
